Colour Strategy

Black & White with 1-Colour

The central idea was 'What would a fancy Watchmaker want as a colour scheme for their website?'

What seemed a natural fit was a strong, but professional text - not too heavy, but formal and classy. As well, a rich gold paired with a stark black/white pairing would emphasize branding.
