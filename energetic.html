Colour Strategy

Complementary

The goal is to try and sell delicious, caffienated, energy drinks to a young audience.

A swooping / casual text, with a forward lean (energetic) seemed like a good choice. As well, fontpair.io recommended a sans-serif font to pair with (Arimo) for easy reading.

Regarding colour, the goal was to pick fruity-colours that pop out to the reader.
