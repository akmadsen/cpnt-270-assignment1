Colour Strategy

Black & White, and One-Colour

This page was intended to be a corporate home or admin page.

Headers are a strong, authoritative, bold display font. Meanwhile, a readable sans-serif approach is used for links and other sections.

The one-colour approach was used to give the air of authority, while emphasizing brand colours. This may be more appropriate for a company admin page.
