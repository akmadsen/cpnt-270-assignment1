Colour Strategy

Analogous

The objective of this page is to advertise relaxing rental cabins.

A soft, round, bold text seemed appropriate for 'relaxation'. Monsterrat was chosen for easy readability and its spaced-out, round design.

Analogous colours were chosen to provide a naturalistic, friendly, earthy feel.
